Guacamole Recipe
4 servings
This super easy guacamole recipe is the perfect dip for any party or gathering. A creamy, vegan and gluten-free Mexican appetizer that requires only little time to prepare.
Ingredients
  • 2 medium avocados
  • 1 small white onion (diced)
  • 2 small tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 small bunch cilantro (chopped)
  • 2 serrano or jalapeño peppers (chopped)
  • 1 large lime (juice)
  • ⅛ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 small garlic clove (peeled)
  • salt (to taste)
Instructions
  1. Place 1 teaspoon salt, garlic, and cumin seeds in a molcajete.
  2. Pestle until you’ll have a paste.
  3. Slice avocados in half, remove the pit and scoop the pulp into the molcajete.
  4. Use the pestle to mash avocados until nicely combined with the garlic paste.
  5. Add onion, tomato, cilantro, and chopped chili peppers.
  6. Add the juice of half a lime and mix everything to combine.
  7. Taste and add more lime juice and salt if needed.
  8. Serve directly in the molcajete.
How to bowl
  1. Slice avocados in half, remove the pit and using a fork scratch the pulp into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the onion, tomato, cilantro, chili pepper and lime juice.
  3. Season with garlic powder, ground cumin and salt. Mix everything to combine, adjust seasonings to taste and serve.
