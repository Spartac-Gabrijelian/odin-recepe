6 Quesadillas
This steak quesadilla recipe is SO DELICIOUS! Seasoned steak, melty cheese, and flavorful sautéed vegetables enclosed within the crispy tortilla. Each mouthful will transport you to a realm of culinary bliss. Enjoy!
Ingredients
  • 1 lb beef loin (cut into thin strips)
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 Tbsp chipotle powder (or smoked paprika)
  • 1 large white onion (sliced)
  • 3 large jalapeño peppers (veins and seeds removed, sliced)
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • salt
Instructions
  1. Place steak in a medium bowl. Add garlic, cumin, pepper, chipotle or paprika powder, and salt. Add oil and mix to coat well the meat.
  2. Cover and allow to marinate for 20 minutes.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan (or comal) over medium heat. Cook meat stirring constantly until no longer pink but there are still liquids in the pan.
  4. Add onions and jalapeño peppers, a splash of water and cook stirring constantly for about 10 minutes or until meat is browned and onions are soft and translucent. Cover and set aside.
Make the quesasillas
  1. Heat an iron cast skillet or comal over medium-low heat. Brush the skillet with a little oil, then place a flour tortilla and top with cheese.
  2. Spoon some of the steak and a little more cheese on top.
  3. Carefully, fold the quesadilla and keep cooking flipping it from time to time until both sides are slightly crispy and browned.
  4. Remove the steak quesadilla from the skillet and cut it in half.
  5. Repeat the steps with all ingredients until they’re gone. Serve with your favorite salsa and toppings.
