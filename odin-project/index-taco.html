tacos Recipe
16 tacos
Tacos al pastor are known for their flavorful taste and are a favorite in Mexico. The perfect balance of smokiness from the roasting meat and the tanginess from the marinade is something you’ll love!
Ingredients
  • 2.2 lb Pork shoulder (cut into 1-inch chunks)
  • 5 Guajillo chilies (stem and seeds removed)
  • 2 Ancho chilies (stem and seeds removed)
  • 3 Garlic cloves (peeled)
  • 2 Whole cloves
  • ½ teaspoon Black peppercorns
  • ½ teaspoon Ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • ½ teaspoon thyme
  • 1 Tablespoons Achiote paste (see notes)
  • 1 cup Pineapple juice
  • Pork lard (or any oil you prefer)
  • Salt

    • For tacos

  • 16 small Corn Tortillas (see notes)
  • 1 cup Pineapple (diced, see notes)
  • 1 medium White Onion (chopped)
  • 1 bunch Fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 2-3 Limes
  • Salsa (see notes)
Instructions
  1. Place the chilies in a bowl with hot water. Soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Drain the chilies and place them into a blender with achiote, garlic, pepper, whole cloves, cumin, thyme, and oregano.
  3. Add pineapple juice and blend until smooth.
  4. In a large bowl place the meat and add salt (about 2 teaspoons).
  5. Pour in the chili sauce and coat evenly turning the meat with tongs.
  6. Cover with cling film and leave meat to marinate preferably overnight, or at least 2 hours (see notes).
  7. Cut the pineapple into chunks the same size as the meat.
  8. Thread the marinated pork chunks into the skewers placing one chunk of pineapple at both ends of each skewer.

    Cook the meat

  1. Heat a grill until smoky and then cook the meat from all sides until nicely charred and cooked through. It will take from 20 to 25 minutes, turning over the skewers as needed.
  2. Once all meat is cooked, take a skewer standing up and slice it into small bites with a sharp knife.
  3. Cut into bites also the pineapple chunks and keep everything well covered to prevent the ingredients from drying out.
Assemble Tacos
  1. Heat the tortillas on the same grill until soft and pliable.
  2. Make tacos placing first the meat in the middle of a tortilla.
  3. Top with chopped onions, cilantro, and pineapple bites.
  4. Pour in your favorite sauce and a squeeze of lime. Enjoy!

Serving: 1 taco | Calories: 218kcal
